Truth Table Generator
This website is for generating truth tables of propositional formulas. Variables should be entered as lowercase letters. The following connectives are supported: ! for negation, | for disjunction (or), & for conjunction (and), -> for implication, <-> for biconditional. If parentheses are not entered, the order of preference of operations is: ! , & , | , -> , <->. An example of a formula is: p & q <-> (r -> !s).
